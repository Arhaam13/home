Almost all cardists bring out their own decks of cards, which means there are so many choices to choose from, and we will help you cut down on which you want to use. Here are the top 14 decks of all time (quality- wise for magic purposes):

Poker 808 Bicycle Cards

This first choice may seem incredibly obvious, but Bicycle does have some of the most high-quality, decent cards on the market. These Poker 808 playing cards are the standard choice from Bicycle that you would see most often in red and blue.They are a tested and loved the pack, with customers raving about how incredibly durable they are even though they are thin and easy to shuffle. The cards have a glossy finish on them, making them stronger and less resistant to stains from liquids. On top of that, they are said to take quite a while to lose their snap – customers had mentioned in online reviews that they got over 90-100 hours before it was lost. These playing cards are also very affordable. You can buy a two-pack combo (comes with both a red and blue deck) on Amazon.

Pros

  1. Durrable
  2. High- quality
  3. Affordable

Cons

  1. Old-fashion design

Swe deck

Next up, we have the SWE deck. To give you a little background about this deck of playing cards, they are named after the author S.W. Erdnase – he published The Expert at the Card Table in the year 1902. This book told the world how magicians were able to pull off their sleight of hand and is now a staple for anyone in the magic community. These cards have the entire cover of the book on the back of them. The cards are a rich and vibrant green and gold, and very easy to read. They are also a fantastic conversation starter when using them around other people. They are a limited-edition card from Ellusionist, so they will be a little more difficult to purchase than the Bicycle cards we just looked at.

Pros

  1. Rich and vibrent design
  2. Limited- edition

Cons

  1. Difficult to purchase
